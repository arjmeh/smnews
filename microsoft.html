Microsoft expected to shut down after stock market crash on February 15, 2022

Microsoft announced that they might have to shut down their company after
a huge stock market crash in Dow Jones. Microsoft stocks fell by 456.31%
and are currently at $0.34 per share. Microsoft is in serious danger and has
to act quick before it's too late. They seeked help from competitors like Apple,
to fund them, but Apple CEO Tim Cook, refused. Bill Gates even came back
to make sure that Microsoft wouldn't shut down. Hopefully Microsoft can get
back up, but many experts doubt a positive outcome.

"I hope we can escape this horrible experience and get back on track with the rest of the market soon." - Bill Gates

"I didn't offer to help Microsoft after they betrayed us in 2017. I hope they can get back up but I doubt they will." - Tim Cook
