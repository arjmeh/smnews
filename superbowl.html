The Ram's Didn't Actually Win Super Bowl 56

Evidence shows that the Ram's actually didn't win Super Bowl 56. On February 13, 2022 Super Bowl 56 was played. In the last 2 minutes of the game, the Rams scored a touchdown. But there has been a lot of controversy regarding this touchdown. You see, they scored exactly 2 yards away from the touchdown line. But many don't realize that it was actually a false start and they didn't score. If this evidence is true, the Rams would have lost Super Bowl 56 by 4 points. This evidence was reported from the NYC police on February 14. Joe Burrow stated: "I was dissapointed when I realized that the Rams didn't score. We should have won and if it was a false start, I would have that ring on my hand."
