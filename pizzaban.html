Pizza being banned in China and Japan. Why?

Authorities announced that the pizza ban in China and Japan was getting worse.
"Ever since a gang of teenagers were making a mess because they threw pizzas,
China and Japan goverment officals have banned all pizza. Anyone caught
eating pizza could be sent to a bootcamp or to jail." says investigator.
The teenagers were making a mess by throwing pizza on the buildings in the
capital of China and in Tokyo, Japan. The teenagers were spotted on camera
and were sent to jail for almost a decade. Many people still don't know about this
ban and are being sent to jail rapidly.

"We must stop this child-play and help make China a better place." - Xi Jinping

"The people have gone too far, we must stop this before it is too late." - Abe Shinzo
